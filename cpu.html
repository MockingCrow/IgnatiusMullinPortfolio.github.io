CPU: Cat Processing Unit

Backend Development

CPU is a cute 2d game based around unique piston mechanics and the interesting ways they can interact to create puzzles. Using pistons, sticky pistons and blocks the player must push a cat from its starting point to a goal by utilizing the complex interactions of different pistons. The project was accepted into the 2022 USC Game Expo.

USC Games Expo 2022

Contribution
This project was produced over a period of 5 months by a two-man team. This time, I took a step out of my comfort zone and handled the art and animation for the project. I created the cat sprite, pistons sprites, the puzzle board and did the animations for the blocks, pistons, and cat, additionally, I did art for the main menu. The art was done using Aesprite and then imported into Unity, where the game was created.
